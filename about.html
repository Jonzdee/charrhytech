Our Mission

Technology That Moves Business

NexCore was founded in 2020 by a group of senior engineers who were tired of watching clients get burned by agencies that overpromised and underdelivered.

We set out to build something different: a studio where technical excellence and honest communication were non-negotiable. Five years later, we've shipped 50+ products across fintech, healthtech, e-commerce, logistics, and more.

We're not the biggest studio in the room. But we're the most committed ones at the table.

How We Work Together

🌐 Remote-First, Async-Smart

Our team operates across multiple time zones. We've invested heavily in async workflows so no one is blocked waiting for a meeting that could have been a message.

📚 Always Learning

Every team member has a dedicated learning budget and dedicated time each week for professional development. The best engineers never stop being students.

💬 Honest Communication

We have a strict no-BS culture. Feedback flows freely, and we'd rather have an awkward conversation early than a failed project late.

🏆 Ship, Reflect, Improve

After every project we do a detailed retrospective. Every lesson learned is turned into a process improvement so we consistently get better.
